Our Journey

Crafting Joy Through Thoughtful Curation

Founded in 2024 by passion-driven curators Anjali, Floralia emerged from a simple belief: gifting should be an art form. What started as a small home-based operation has grown into a trusted source for beautifully curated hamper experiences that touch hearts and celebrate moments.

Today, our dedicated team continues to hand-select every item with care, combining premium products with elegant presentation. Each hamper tells a story and creates a memory that lasts far beyond the unboxing moment.

Anjali Unnikrishnan
